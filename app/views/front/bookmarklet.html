Add pins to your ParentPins Boards from any website or import your Pinterest pins

Add the ParentPin It Button

  • Make sure your bookmarks bar is open in your browser.
  • Then, simply drag and drop this <%= bookmarklet_link %> link to your bookmarks bar.
  • Now when you are on a website, click the button in your bookmarks bar to create a pin from the url you are on.
  • If you want to import you existing Pinterest pins, log into pinterest, go to your boards page or a specific board, and click the ParentPin It Button in your bookmarks bar to start the import process.
