Add Pins to Your ParentPins Boards Using the ParentPins bookmarklet you can add pins from any website, or easily import your Pinterest pins onto your ParentPins boards.

How to add the ParentPin It! button:

  • Make sure your bookmarks bar is open in your browser.
  • Then, simply drag and drop the link below to your bookmarks bar. <%= bookmarklet_link %>
  • Now when you are on a website, click the button in your bookmarks bar to create a pin from the url you are on.

How to use the ParentPin It! button to import your Pinterest pins:

  • f you want to import your existing Pinterest pins, first log into your Pinterest account, go to your boards page or a specific board, and then click the ParentPin It! button in your bookmarks bar to start the import process.
